Explore Blackpool

Blackpool is a famous seaside town in the north‑west of England. It is located on the coast of the Irish Sea and has been a popular holiday destination for many years. People come to Blackpool to enjoy the beach, entertainment, and exciting attractions.

Blackpool became popular in the 1800s when trains made it easy for people to visit the seaside. It quickly grew into one of the UK’s best-known tourist resorts. Today, millions of visitors come each year. Some of the most popular attractions include Blackpool Tower, the Pleasure Beach, and the Blackpool Illuminations.
